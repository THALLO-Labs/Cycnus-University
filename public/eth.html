About the course
The course includes everything from cryptography to smart contracts to fully functional dApp development. This is the #1 resource to jumpstart your web3 career and a MUST if you want to learn Ethereum.

The curriculum includes interactive coding challenges, videos, written guides, and weekly projects - as well as a capstone final project which can be submitted for official Alchemy certification.

This bootcamp, along with the rest of the AU content, is supported by the AU community of learners, builders and entreprenuers - join the AU Discord for the max bootcamp experience!
